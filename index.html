About MQCQC


Mary the Queen College of Quezon City formerly as Mary the Queen College of Science and Technology started in 2003 with caregiving and other health allied courses. The vision focused in establishing an educational institution providing "Academic Excelence and Integral Formation" for the underprivileged, but deserving students into higher level of education. Starting year 2007, it has added courses such as BS Information Systems, BS Hotel and Restaurant Management, BS Information Technology, Bachelor of Elementary Education, and BS Accounting Technology. In 2009, the institution tied up with government agencies such as the Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA) and thus MQC-Carer Institute was founded, as the technical vocational arm of MQC and ofered short vocational courses.
